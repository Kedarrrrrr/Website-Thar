The Mahindra Thar offers a captivating feature like a retractable soft top roof, enabling drivers to indulge in the exhilarating experience of open-top driving. The 2023 Thar's ability to provide such an immersive and enjoyable open-top experience further enhances its appeal among adventurous individuals seeking unforgettable moments on the road. Renowned for its outstanding off-roading prowess, the new Thar stands tall among its peers. With a sturdy build, generous ground clearance and a reliable 4x4 drivetrain, it effortlessly conquers formidable terrains encompassing muddy tracks, rocky paths and steep inclines.
